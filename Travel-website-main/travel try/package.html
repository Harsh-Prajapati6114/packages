Tour Package Details

Maldives

Discover opulent overwater bungalows and secluded beachfront villas, meticulously selected to ensure privacy and extravagance.

Here’s an example of a Maldives tour package within a budget of ₹80,000 INR, tailored for a 3-night, 4-day stay:

  • INCLUSIONS/EXCLUSIONS
  • PICKUP POINT

1. Hotel Accommodation:

- Hotel: Kaani Village & Spa (Maafushi Island, South Male Atoll)
- Accommodation Type: Standard rooms with all basic amenities.

Facilities:

- Air-conditioning, free Wi-Fi, private balcony, and access to a spa and wellness center.
- Free access to the hotel’s private beach area.
- On-site restaurant offering local and international cuisines.

2. Package Inclusions:

- Duration: 3 Nights / 4 Days

Meals:

- Complimentary breakfast for all 3 days.
- Optional lunch/dinner at the hotel (at an additional cost).

Transfers:

- Round-trip speedboat transfers from Male International Airport to Maafushi Island (included in the package).

Activities:

- Snorkeling Excursion: A guided tour to nearby coral reefs.
- Access to Water Sports: Enjoy activities like kayaking, paddleboarding, etc.
- Beach Access: Relax and enjoy the hotel's private beach

3. Estimated Cost:

- Per Person: Approx. ₹39,000 INR
- Total for Two People: ₹78,000 INR (This may vary slightly based on the room category and booking site).

4. Flights:

- Flights from major Indian cities like Delhi, Mumbai, or Bangalore to Male (not included in the package).
- The average round-trip cost is around ₹25,000–₹30,000 per person, depending on the airline and booking time.

5. Visa:

- Indian passport holders get a free 30-day visa on arrival in the Maldives, so no extra cost for visa processing.

6. Additional Information:

- Best Time to Visit: November to April (Dry season, with great weather and clear skies).
- Booking Platforms: MakeMyTrip, Thomas Cook, or directly through hotel websites like Kaani Village & Spa.

Important Tips:

- Book early to avail the best discounts on accommodation and flight tickets.
- Check for any seasonal offers or last-minute deals, especially during non-peak months.

This package gives a great balance of relaxation and adventure while staying within your budget of ₹80,000 INR.
